4 Hour Venture Video Hack (TOA Satelite)

Rainmaking Loft, Charlottenstr. 2, Berlin

About Event

4 hour venture is an event format designed to validate ideas and built web-prototypes in a very short time period. Our Video Hack Edition targets the digital entertainment industry, trying to find new ways of video usage, interactivity and business solutions. We provide a framework that allows you to accelerate ideas and test assumptions super quickly. You can bring your own ideas or join another idea and try to push that idea the next hours as hard as you can. We love to see prototypes - so use frameworks and tools that get you far in the short time. Then you pitch your idea and the work you did in front of our jury and win some cool prizes! We connect developers, designers and content people and we would love to lift this whole thing to an entrepreneurial level. The outcome might be a prototype for a new YouTube Extension, a Multiscreen App, a Content Aggregator or Real Time Video Editing Tools. Or something totally different no one ever thought of. Join us to create the future of video!
